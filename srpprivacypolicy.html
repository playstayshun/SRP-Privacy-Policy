Route Planner

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

01

Overview

Route Planner ("the App") is a mobile application that helps you plan and optimise multi-stop routes on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

02

Information We Collect

The App requests access to the following on your device:

  • Precise Location (GPS) — used only when you choose to add your current location as a stop. Location data is never stored on any server or transmitted beyond the third-party routing services listed below.
  • Addresses you enter — stop addresses typed by you are sent to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service for geocoding (converting an address to coordinates). They are not stored by us.
  • Route coordinates — stop coordinates are sent to the OSRM routing engine to calculate an optimised route order. They are not stored by us.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, payment details, or device identifiers to our own servers. The App has no user accounts and no backend database.

03

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your current location as an optional starting stop.
  • To geocode addresses you enter into map coordinates.
  • To calculate and display an optimised multi-stop route.
  • To open your chosen stop address in your device's default maps application.

Location and address data is used exclusively within a single session to provide routing functionality. No data is used for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

04

Data Storage

All App data (stop lists, preferences) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

We do not operate any servers that store user data.

05

Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following third-party services to function. Each service has its own privacy policy:

OpenStreetMap — Nominatim

Geocoding service. Address strings you type are sent to Nominatim to retrieve GPS coordinates. OpenStreetMap's servers are located in the EU.

osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy ↗

Project OSRM

Open-source routing engine. Stop coordinates are sent to OSRM's public demo server to calculate the optimised route order. No addresses or personal details are included in these requests.

project-osrm.org ↗

Google Maps / Device Maps App

When you tap a stop to open it in maps, the address or coordinates are passed to your device's default maps application (e.g. Google Maps, Waze). That app's own privacy policy applies at that point.

policies.google.com/privacy ↗

Google Fonts & Leaflet (CDN)

Fonts and map tile assets may be loaded from Google Fonts and unpkg.com CDN. Standard CDN access logs (IP address, timestamp) may be retained by those services per their own policies. No personal App data is shared with them.

Google Fonts Privacy ↗

06

App Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — requested at runtime only when you use the "Use My Location" feature. You can deny this permission; the App will still work with manually entered addresses.
  • INTERNET — required to reach the geocoding and routing APIs described above and to load map tiles.

07

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the details below and we will take appropriate action.

08

Your Rights

Because the App stores no personal data on our servers, there is no data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All locally stored data can be removed by clearing the App's storage in your Android device settings (Settings → Apps → Route Planner → Storage → Clear Data) or by uninstalling the App.

If you have questions about data held by any third-party service listed above, please refer to that service's own privacy policy and data request procedures.
